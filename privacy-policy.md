# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 22 July 2026

The Lab Manual Builder stores and manages your content using the Open Science Framework (OSF).

## Information We Collect

When you use the application, we may access information needed to authenticate you with OSF, such as your OSF account identifier. We also store the lab manuals and other content that you create using the application.

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

* authenticate your account;
* save, retrieve, and manage your lab manuals on OSF; and
* operate and improve the application.

We do not sell your personal information or use it for advertising.

## Data Storage

Your lab manuals and associated files are stored on OSF. The handling of data stored on OSF is also subject to the OSF Privacy Policy.

## Sharing

We do not share your personal information except where necessary to provide the service (for example, by interacting with OSF) or where required by law.

## Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no online service can guarantee absolute security.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the application maintainers.
